Get Started with Monero

Simple, safe step-by-step guide for beginners (January 2026). Always verify everything from official sources!

  1. Download a Wallet
    Go to the official site: getmonero.org/downloads.
    Choose the **GUI Wallet** (easiest for most people) or Feather Wallet (lightweight, fast).
    Download for your OS (Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, etc.).
  2. Install & Create Your Wallet
    Run the installer → Select "Create a new wallet".
    **Very important:** Write down your 25-word seed phrase on paper and store it offline (never take a photo or save digitally!). This is your only way to recover your funds if something goes wrong.
  3. Sync the Blockchain
    The wallet needs to download the full Monero blockchain (this can take several hours or even days the first time).
    For a faster start, you can use a **remote node** (in wallet settings → enter a public node like node.moneroworld.com:18089).
    Later, run your own node for maximum privacy (see "Download Node" button on dashboard).
  4. Buy XMR Privately (Recommended: No KYC)
    To keep your privacy, avoid centralized exchanges.
    Best options right now (2026):
    • Haveno → decentralized P2P (haveno.exchange)
    • Bisq → fully open-source P2P (bisq.network)
    • Atomic swaps (BTC → XMR directly in wallet)
    Start with a small amount to test the process.
  5. Secure & Start Using It
    Run your own node when possible.
    Use Tor or I2P for extra connection privacy.
    Test everything: Send a tiny amount to yourself or a friend first.
    Join the community on Reddit (r/Monero) or Matrix for help if needed.
Key Privacy Rules:
• Never store large amounts on exchanges — they track your activity.
• Always self-custody your XMR (you control the keys).
• Verify every download with PGP signatures from the official site.
• Keep your seed phrase safe and offline forever.
